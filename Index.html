Shot Stories(@shotstories4u)

Hi!

"Shot Stories" was born due to two playful creative minds on June, 2020 amidst the pangs of pandemic. They present original stories in the form of Bengali audio stories, that are inspired from the patches of life. All these stories are on-the-go-tales that can be enjoyed at any point of time, with only ears at them. They have tremendous power to touch the audience with one single stroke.
